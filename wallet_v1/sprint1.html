Terms of Use
Read and accept our terms to continue setting up your wallet.

1. Self-Custody Wallet. Wallet is a non-custodial application. You are solely responsible for securing your Secret Recovery Phrase and private keys. We do not store, transmit, or have access to your keys at any time.

2. No Warranty. This software is provided "as is" without warranty. Cryptocurrency transactions are irreversible. Verify all recipient addresses before sending funds.

3. Seed Phrase Security. Your Secret Recovery Phrase is the master key to your funds. Anyone who obtains it can take your funds permanently. Write it on paper, store it physically secure, and never share it — not even with Wallet support.

4. Regulatory Compliance. You are responsible for complying with applicable laws in your jurisdiction, including tax and AML obligations.

5. No Financial Advice. Nothing here constitutes financial, investment, legal, or tax advice. Token values are volatile. Conduct your own research.
