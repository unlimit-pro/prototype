    From analytics tool to institutional intelligence platform — in 24 months.

    Unlimit starts where most crypto dashboards stop — basic price and wallet data — and builds systematically toward a fully autonomous, AI-powered intelligence layer that institutions and professional traders rely on daily.

    The four phases are deliberately sequenced: each phase's business outcomes (users, revenue, clients) fund and justify the engineering investment of the next. By Month 24, the platform is self-sustaining, not dependent on external funding.

    Multi-chain expansion (EVM, Aptos, SUI) researched and ready for Phase 5 — targeting a total addressable market 10× the size of Solana alone.
