Achieved Goal — What Can Users Do With It?

By the end of each phase, here is what a user of Unlimit Intelligent System is able to do — the concrete, daily-use capabilities unlocked.

👁
Track Any Token or Wallet
Users can add any Solana token or wallet address to a personalised Watchlist and receive real-time price, volume, and on-chain activity alerts without needing to visit a block explorer.
🔍
Screen Tokens by Whale Activity
Filter and rank thousands of tokens by Whale Score, liquidity depth, social sentiment, and launch age — surfacing high-conviction opportunities in seconds, not hours.
📊
Analyse DEX Pools & Liquidity
View real-time TVL, 24h volume, APY, impermanent loss risk, and pool health scores across all major Solana DEXs from a single aggregated dashboard.
🔔
Build & Fire Custom Alerts
Create multi-condition alert rules (price threshold + whale accumulation + sentiment spike) and receive push, email, or in-app notifications the moment conditions are met — with a full trigger history.
🤖
Run Auto-DCA Strategies
Schedule recurring buy orders for any token with configurable intervals, amounts, and stop conditions. View live countdown to next execution, pause/resume at any time, and review a full execution history.
🐋
Follow Smart Money & Whales
Detect coordinated whale accumulation, view the Smart Money Leaderboard, and get a Consensus Score showing how many top wallets are positioned on the same token.
💼
Track Portfolio P&L in Real Time
Connect multiple wallets and see a consolidated portfolio with realised/unrealised P&L, historical equity chart, token allocation breakdown, and one-click export to CSV.
📈
Backtest & Compare Strategies
Run historical simulations of DCA, trend-follow, and signal-based strategies. Compare equity curves side-by-side with Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, and win-rate metrics.
🔁
Copy-Trade Top Wallets
Browse the Copy Trading Marketplace, subscribe to any verified trader, set position-size limits and stop-loss thresholds, and have trades mirrored on-chain automatically — with a full performance report.
🧠
Get AI-Powered Insights
Receive LLM-generated one-line summaries per token, a personalised AI Strategy Advisor suggesting allocation changes, an AI News Curator filtering macro noise, and an Insights Feed ranked by relevance.
🌐
Access via API or Mobile
Developers and institutions can query all data through a documented REST/WebSocket API with an interactive Playground. End users can access the full platform on iOS and Android with offline caching.
🔬
Deep-Dive Token Research
View contract audit scores, holder distribution charts, KOL mentions, on-chain transaction history, and social sentiment — all in a single Token Research Hub — to make fully informed entry decisions.

Overall Result Summary

Unlimit becomes the institutional intelligence standard for Solana — and the foundation for multi-chain leadership.

At the end of 24 months, Unlimit Intelligent System is no longer a prototype or an analytics dashboard — it is a fully operational, revenue-generating intelligence platform used by 200,000+ individual traders and 5+ institutional clients, with a live public API, native mobile apps, and a white-label B2B product line.

The platform has replaced the need for 8–10 separate tools (block explorer, DEX screener, portfolio tracker, alert service, copy-trading platform, news aggregator, strategy backtester, and API data provider) with a single, coherent product. This consolidation is the primary value proposition and the primary driver of both retention and pricing power.

The $2M+ MRR target — achieved through a four-tier revenue model (Free, Pro, Enterprise, API + White-Label) — places the business on a clear path to profitability without requiring additional venture funding, and positions it for a Series A raise from a position of strength rather than necessity.

The multi-chain expansion research completed in Phase 4 means the platform is technically ready to extend beyond Solana in a Phase 5, targeting the EVM ecosystem (Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum), Aptos, and SUI — a total addressable market roughly 10× larger than Solana alone.

Note on Descoped Items: Community Leaderboard (IS-436) and Portfolio Tax Summary (IS-437) were descoped during Sprint 25 to focus engineering capacity on higher-priority items (real-time price feed, copy-trade execution, DEX aggregator). These may be re-introduced in a post-Month-24 roadmap cycle if user demand justifies the investment.
